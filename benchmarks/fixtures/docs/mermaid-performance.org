* Reading interval

The benchmark keeps real document prose between diagrams so that later figures
exercise intersection-driven rendering instead of being rendered eagerly.

The documents theme must keep text interactive while optional diagrams are
prepared. Navigation, selection, search, and scrolling cannot wait for Mermaid.

The second paragraph creates a stable reading interval. It also makes the
scenario representative of architecture notes rather than a diagram gallery.
