* Documentation document
:PROPERTIES:
:CONTRACT_ID: org-zhixing.document
:CONTRACT_SCOPE: document
:END:

This document defines the baseline contract applied to every Org document in
=content_dirs=.  Keeping the contract in =docs/= makes the rules readable,
versioned, and subject to their own validation.

** A document has a title
:PROPERTIES:
:ASSERT_ID: document.has-title
:ASSERT_SEVERITY: error
:END:

#+begin_src org-elements-selector
(:org-element (:type keyword :name TITLE))
#+end_src

#+begin_src jinja2 :name message
The Org document has no #+TITLE keyword.
#+end_src

#+begin_src jinja2 :name fix
Add #+TITLE: ${title} at the top of the Org file.
#+end_src
