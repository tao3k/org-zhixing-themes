* PUBLISHED Long-form reading should feel quiet :blog:essay:
CLOSED: [2026-05-15 Fri 22:40]
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: zen-long-form-reading
:SLUG: long-form-reading-quiet
:AREA: writing
:KIND: essay
:END:
Zen mode is not the Blog section itself.  Blog is the library; Zen mode is the
reading posture for one selected article.

The article surface therefore needs enough typography to carry real writing:
paragraph rhythm, headings, footnotes, quotes, lists, and occasional technical
blocks.  The page should not feel like a settings dashboard pretending to be an
essay.

** A calmer outline

Org documents often mix private structure and public prose.  A personal site
should preserve that structure without exposing every internal switch as a UI
gadget.

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Read one note.
Find one next action.
Leave the rest quiet.
#+END_VERSE

** Side notes

The phrase /知行合一/ is a product constraint here: knowledge and action must be
visible in the same source, but not with equal visual volume.  Agenda signals can
exist below an essay without making the essay feel noisy.
